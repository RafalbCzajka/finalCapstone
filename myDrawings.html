My drawings

Unfortunately I wasn't able to complete the challenge but below are the digital drawings that I did create in the first week of the Inktober 2022 drawing challenge. I created these images in Adobe Photoshop by drawing with a mouse as I didn't have access to a drawing tablet, despite these limitations I am happy with how I improved throughout the challenge.

The days of the challenge that I did manage to complete include:

  1. Gargoyle
  2. Scurry
  3. Bat
  4. Scallop
  5. Flame
  6. Bouquet

As an additional rule to the challenge I also tried to use various techniques to include a 'among us' character in each drawing.
